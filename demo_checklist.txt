  Q: "Is this HIPAA compliant?"
  A: "HIPAA-appropriate audit logging is a Week 2 deliverable

      it scores evidence quality, not patient data. The audit
      trail records action type, authority state, and outcome —
      nothing that would require PHI handling."

  Q: "Who else is using this?"

  Q: "How is this different from what Anthropic or OpenAI provide?"
  A: "Their safety layers are intrinsic — baked into model behavior.
      AIiTAPP is extrinsic — it sits above any model from any
      provider and scores the evidence, not the model's output.
      We are provider-agnostic. We do not replace their safety
      layers. We are the governance infrastructure above them."
